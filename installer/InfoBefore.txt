ABOUT THIS SOFTWARE
-------------------
CiviQual (Quality Analytics for Public Service) for Lean Six Sigma
is statistical analysis software designed for public service organizations.

Named in honor of Dr. Gregory H. Watson, a pioneer in quality management.
